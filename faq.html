FAQ

Common questions from small businesses.

What kinds of businesses do you help?

TeeJay's Tech Tools is built for small businesses in Farmington, Missouri and surrounding areas, especially businesses that need help with websites, SEO, Google Business Profiles, CRM systems, advertising flow, and general operational tech problems.

Can you help with my Google Business Profile?

Yes. That includes setup, cleanup, optimization, business details, categories, service updates, and making sure your profile supports local visibility on Google Search and Maps.

Do you build websites?

Yes. TeeJay's Tech Tools builds clean, modern websites and landing pages designed to clearly explain what your business does and make it easier for customers to contact you.

Do you offer SEO help?

Yes. That includes local SEO improvements, better page structure, messaging, keyword-focused content, and updates that help your business show up better when local customers are already searching.

Can you help with CRM or business systems?

Yes. TeeJay has experience working around complex dealership systems and can help small businesses with CRM organization, lead flow, follow-up structure, and making connected systems work better together.

What if I just have a random tech problem in my business?

That is absolutely part of the service. If something in your business is confusing, broken, messy, disconnected, or slowing you down, TeeJay's Tech Tools is meant to help simplify it.
