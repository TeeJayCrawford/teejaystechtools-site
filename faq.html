FAQ

Common questions from local business owners.

What kind of businesses is this best for?

Local service businesses, owner-operated companies, local shops, and small teams that need a clearer online presence and more practical digital support.

Do you only do websites?

No. Websites are a big part of the work, but so are Google Business Profile optimization, Apple Maps setup, local SEO, online stores, and consulting around digital business systems.

Can you help my business show up better on Google?

Yes. That can include your website, service pages, local SEO structure, and Google Business Profile cleanup.

Can you help with Apple Maps too?

Yes. Apple Maps matters more than many business owners think, especially for iPhone users trying to call, navigate, or verify a business quickly.

Do you offer consulting or only done-for-you work?

Both. Some projects are consulting-focused. Some are fully hands-on. Some start with consulting and turn into project work.

Do you work with businesses near Farmington only?

The main focus is Farmington and surrounding Southeast Missouri communities, but some projects can be handled more broadly depending on the fit.
