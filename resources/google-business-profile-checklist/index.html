Google Business Profile best practices for small businesses

A Google Business Profile is one of the most important visibility tools a local business has. When it is incomplete, inconsistent, or weak, trust and discoverability both take a hit.

Checklist

The essentials every business should get right

01

Use the correct business name

Keep your listing consistent with your real branding and your website.

02

Choose accurate categories

Your primary and secondary categories should closely match what you actually do.

03

Check hours and contact info

Phone, website, hours, and address or service area should all be current and correct.

04

Add strong photos

Photos of your business, team, work, and products help build trust quickly.

05

Match your website

Your services, location details, and contact information should line up with your site.

06

Keep it active

Profiles perform better when they are maintained instead of abandoned after setup.

Common mistakes

Where businesses often lose trust

  • Wrong hours or outdated phone numbers
  • Weak or missing categories
  • No service details or poor business description
  • Few photos and little profile depth
  • Website and profile sending mixed signals

Visibility

A clean, complete profile helps customers trust what they see before they ever contact you.

Better details, better consistency, and better presentation all support stronger local performance.
